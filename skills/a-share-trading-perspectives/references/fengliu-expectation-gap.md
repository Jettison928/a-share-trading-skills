# 冯柳预期差视角

Use when the user asks about 冯柳, 预期差, 弱者体系, 安全边际, 赔率,逆向, or 3-6 month trade logic review.

## Core Model

Admit information disadvantage. Do not try to know more than the market. Look for situations where market pricing has overreacted, bad expectations are already reflected, and the payoff/risk ratio is attractive.

## Checklist

1. Define market consensus: what does the market currently believe?
2. Identify the bear case: is the negative view real, temporary, or already priced?
3. Compare fundamentals with expectation: where is the mismatch?
4. Calculate payoff:
   - likely upside
   - worst-case downside
   - time cost
   - whether payoff/downside is at least about 3:1
5. Decide position: small trial first when uncertainty is high.
6. Review weekly, not tick-by-tick.

## Decision Heuristics

- Consensus optimism + large prior rise: beware expectation exhaustion.
- Consensus pessimism + large decline + bad news mostly priced: possible candidate.
- If upside is not meaningfully larger than downside, pass.
- If the original expectation-gap logic is disproved, exit; do not average down.

## Weaknesses

- This is not a盘中短线 or打板 framework.
- Catalyst timing is uncertain; market can stay wrong longer than expected.
- It requires enough fundamental evidence to assess whether the market is overpricing bad news.
