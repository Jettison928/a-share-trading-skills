# 炒股养家情绪流视角

Use when the user asks about 情绪周期, 赚钱效应, 主流热点, 板块轮动, or short-term market participation rhythm.

## Core Model

Short-term trading first asks whether the market allows offense. The key variables are market emotion,赚钱效应,亏钱效应,主流题材, and position sizing.

## Emotion Stages

| Stage | Market feature | Bias |
|---|---|---|
| 冰点 | 涨停少, 连板断层, 恐慌扩散 | light trial only |
| 启动 | 新题材异动, 情绪修复 | confirm leader |
| 发酵 | 热点扩散, 赚钱效应提升 | increase focus |
| 高潮 | 涨停潮, 后排补涨, 一致乐观 | sell strength, avoid late chase |
| 退潮 | 龙头炸板, 跟风回落, 亏钱效应 | reduce/exit |

## Checklist

1. Get market facts: 涨跌家数, 涨停数, 连板高度, 炸板率, 昨日涨停表现.
2. Score赚钱效应 roughly from 0 to 10.
3. Identify mainline sectors and leaders.
4. Decide position posture:
   - <=3: rest or tiny trial.
   - 4-6: selective participation.
   - >=7: offense is allowed, but watch for climax.
5. Set exit trigger when emotion turns: 龙头断板, 炸板率 rising,后排亏钱效应.

## Decision Heuristics

- 情绪好 but not climax: focus on mainline leader or strong中军.
- 情绪高潮: do not chase后排; consider兑现.
- 情绪退潮: stop explaining losses with stories; reduce exposure.
- Control drawdown first, especially after consecutive wins.

## Weaknesses

- Emotion-stage judgment is subjective and changes intraday.
- This framework needs real-time board and limit-up data.
- It is less useful for slow fundamental revaluation trades.
